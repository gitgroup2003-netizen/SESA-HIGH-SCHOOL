About SESA High School

A Legacy of Excellence

SESA High School is a premier educational institution located in Kassanda district, deeply committed to nurturing intellectual competence, creative thinking, and strong moral character in our young leaders.

Our holistic approach to education ensures that every student develops not only academically but also personally, ethically, and socially. We believe in preparing leaders who will drive positive change in Uganda and beyond.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, we provide an environment where excellence is not just encouraged—it's expected and celebrated.

Admission Requirements

Academic Standards

A minimum aggregate of Division 3 or equivalent in Primary Leaving Examinations or O-Levels.

Character Reference

A conduct certificate from your previous school confirming good moral standing.

Medical Certificate

A current medical examination confirming good health status.

Personal Interview

Selected applicants will be invited for an interview with school leadership.

Tuition & Fees

Flexible payment plans available. Contact admissions office for detailed fee structure.
